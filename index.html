Halwa Puri And Cholay Ka Salan

Recipe by Chef Mah Rukh

Ingredients

Puri
  • Flour 2 cups (Makes about 20)
  • Oil 2 tsp
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

Poori
  1. Make the dough with flour and 2 tsp oil and water as needed.
  2. Make into small puris (pancake like) as flat as you can and deep fry in hot oil.

Cholay

Ingredients

  • Presoaked whole channa 1/2 tsp
  • Salt to taste
  • Zera Powder 1/2tsp
  • Crushed Whole dried red chilies1/2
  • Haldi Powder 1/4 tsp

Instructions

Cholay
  1. Boil the channa with salt until soft.
  2. In a pot heat 1 tsp of oil.
  3. Add spices and softened channa and 1/4 cup of water.
  4. Mix and mash slightly.
  5. Cook till bubbles appear then remove from heat.

Halwa

Ingredients

  • Sujji (semolina) 1 cup
  • Oil 2 tsp
  • Sugar 1-1/2cup
  • Water 1/2
  • Seeds of small Illaichi 3
  • Yellow food color (optional)

Instructions

Halwa
  1. Put the sujji and oil in a pot along with Illaichi seeds and fry until slightly brown on very low heat.
  2. Meanwhile combine sugar and water to make a syrup.
  3. Add this to the sujji taking care not to burn yourself and cook on low heat to desired consistency.
  4. If you want to color the halwa add the color to the syrup before pouring it into the sujji.
